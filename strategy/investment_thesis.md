# V7 Capital - Investment Strategy

## Asymmetric Risk / Returns

### Listed Securities & IPOs (40–60%)

| Criteria | Target |
|----------|--------|
| **Type** | Heavily discounted businesses (asset-based or operational cash flows) |
| **Alternative** | Fairly valued with strong, highly probable growth prospects |
| **Enhancement** | Tactical yield (e.g., short covered call options) |
| **Target return** | min. 15% IRR |
| **Risk** | Limited downside |

### Pre-IPO / Private Equity (30–40%)

| Criteria | Target |
|----------|--------|
| **Business model** | Robust, profitable (high margins), proven track record |
| **Debt** | Low or no debt (except financial services) |
| **Growth** | High growth potential leveraged by invested capital (significant ROIC) |
| **Valuation** | Discounted or fair (with ratchets and earnouts) |
| **Target return** | min. 25% IRR |
| **Risk** | Limited downside |

### Venture / Startups (10–15%)

| Criteria | Target |
|----------|--------|
| **Business model** | Clear value-added in sizable market/industry |
| **Exit** | Foreseeable exit path |
| **Stage** | Early stage at very low valuations |
| **Opportunity** | Fair valuations for exponential opportunities (internationalization potential) |
| **Target return** | min. 10x MOIC |
| **Risk** | Very high downside |

---

## Risk Mitigation

1. Tail risk hedging (put options based on portfolio structure)
2. Available cash for dollar cost averaging
3. Diversification
4. Tactical options hedging
5. Careful DD and deal structuring
6. Syndicated investments with larger (international) funds
7. **Small bets rule:** Potential total loss of venture area covered by 2–3 years of attributable profits from other two areas
