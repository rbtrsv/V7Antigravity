# V7 Capital - Investment Strategy Overview

## 1. Executive Summary
**Vision:** To be the leading catalyst for sustainable growth and innovation in the CEE region.
**Mission:** Deploy capital intelligently across varying asset classes to achieve asymmetric risk-adjusted returns while empowering visionary founders.

---

## 2. Investment Thesis & Allocation
> *See detailed breakdown in [Investment Thesis](investment_thesis.md)*

Our strategy focuses on a balanced portfolio approach:
- **Listed Securities & IPOs (40-60%)**: High liquidity, steady returns, deep value.
- **Private Equity / Pre-IPO (30-40%)**: Growth capital, proven business models, clear path to exit.
- **Venture Capital (10-15%)**: High-risk/high-reward bets on disruptive technologies and early-stage opportunities.

**Key Sectors**: Agribusiness, Wellness, Education, Energy Efficiency, Consumer Goods.

---

## 3. Investment Criteria
> *See detailed checklist in [Investment Criteria](criteria.md)*

We adhere to strict selection criteria to ensure alignment and return potential:
*   **Alignment**: Shared values, integrity, and long-term vision.
*   **Business Quality**: Strong moats, scalable models, and unit economics.
*   **Market**: Significant Total Addressable Market (TAM) with growth tailwinds.
*   **Deal Structure**: Protective provisions, clear valuation logic, and alignment of incentives.

---

## 4. Deal Flow & Process
> *Track current opportunities in [Pipeline](pipeline.md)*

### 4.1 Sourcing strategy
- Proprietary network & referrals
- Strategic partnerships
- Inbound screenings

### 4.2 Decision Process
1.  **Screening**: Quick filter against core mandates.
2.  **Deep Dive / DD**: Commercial, Financial, Legal, and Tech diligence.
3.  **Investment Committee (IC)**: Final approval and terms finalization.
4.  **Closing & Onboarding**: Execution and 100-day plan implementation.

---

## 5. Portfolio Value Creation (Post-Investment)
* "Hands-on" strategic guidance.
* Operational support (recruiting, financial planning).
* Business development & networking access.
* Follow-on funding preparation.

---

## 6. Exit Strategy
* **Strategic Acquisitions (M&A)**: Selling to strategic players in the sector.
* **Secondary Sales**: Exiting to PE funds or other financial sponsors.
* **IPOs**: For mature portfolio companies.
* **Buybacks**: Founder buybacks where appropriate detailed in the SHA.
