# V7 Capital - Fund Structure

## Fund Outline

| Element | Detalii |
|---------|---------|
| **Maturity** | Evergreen; very long-term focus |
| **Size / AUM** | No specific target size, raising capital based on opportunities |
| **Min. ticket** | 1% of AUM |
| **Raises** | One (max. 2) per year |

## Capital Allocation

| Category | Allocation |
|----------|------------|
| Listed Securities & IPOs | 40–60% |
| Pre-IPO / Private Equity | 30–40% |
| Venture (Startups) | 10–15% |

## Investment Approach

- **Diversified, agnostic** – doar businessuri cu economics pe care le înțelegem complet
- **Focus:** IPOs, pre-IPOs, capital markets
- **Opportunistic:** venture deals (startups)
- **Minority stakes** în companii locale conduse de antreprenori committed, competent și ethical

## Leverage Policy

- Posibil, dar improbabil
- Based on financial market conditions, deal type și portfolio structure
- **Debt limits:** max 15% of AUM la nivel de portofoliu, max 5x EBITDA per deal

## Risk / Returns

- **Targeting asymmetric payoffs**
- Max deal size = 15% of AUM
- Tactical hedging when/where possible
- **Target:** min. 15% net IRR → 2x every 5 years, after costs
